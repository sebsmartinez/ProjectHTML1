User Testimonials

Reviewer: bhonhoff

Strengths:

  • Limited lens range. (In der Begrenzung zeigt sich der Meister) Limitations show whether you are a real master or just fake.
  • Silent (but not as silent as my Lubitel II).
  • Focusing spot on (compared to digital cameras, except a Leica M8 or M9).

Weaknesses:

  • Film loading with a quickload spool is slower than the standard 'N' spool.
  • No TTL (on the M2). Awkward use for macro (using a Visoflex 1 or Visoflex 2).

Reviewer: Michael J Hoffman

Strengths:

  • A rangefinder is a somewhat specialized tool.
  • The viewing system with regard to being able to see what will be recorded on the film inside the framelines...

Weaknesses:

  • Somewhat limited application framing not as accurate as with an SLR limited range of useful focal lengths.
