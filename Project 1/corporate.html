Corporate Information

Leica Camera AG, headquartered in the picturesque town of Wetzlar, Germany, stands as a beacon of excellence in the realm of optical and photographic equipment. Founded in 1914 by the visionary Ernst Leitz, the company has etched its name into the annals of photographic history, becoming synonymous with precision, innovation, and craftsmanship.

As a trailblazer in the industry, Leica has continuously pushed the boundaries of imaging technology. The Leica M3, introduced in 1954, is a masterpiece that exemplifies the company's dedication to precision engineering. This iconic rangefinder camera not only revolutionized the photography landscape but also set the stage for the legendary Leica M series.

Leica's product range encompasses a spectrum of cutting-edge cameras, lenses, and sport optics. From the revered Leica M series rangefinders to the innovative Leica SL mirrorless cameras and compact digital cameras, each product bears the hallmark of exceptional build quality, optical performance, and timeless design.

With a global presence, Leica Camera AG has become a symbol of photographic excellence, embraced by professionals, enthusiasts, and collectors across continents. The brand's boutiques and authorized dealers serve as gateways to a world where precision meets passion, offering customers access to the latest innovations and expert service.

Leica's corporate philosophy centers on precision, quality, and innovation. The company strives to create products that deliver unparalleled imaging experiences, seamlessly blending state-of-the-art technology with the legacy of traditional craftsmanship. The Leica legacy continues to inspire, leaving an indelible mark on the art and practice of photography.

Founding and Legacy:

Leica Camera AG was founded in 1914 by Ernst Leitz in Wetzlar, Germany. The name "Leica" is derived from the combination of "Leitz" and "camera," reflecting the company's founder. Leica has a rich legacy in the field of photography, pioneering innovations such as 35mm film format and rangefinder technology.

Product Range:

Leica is globally recognized for its premium cameras, lenses, and sport optics. The company's product range includes the iconic Leica M series rangefinder cameras, the innovative Leica SL mirrorless cameras, compact digital cameras, and a comprehensive line of precision lenses. Leica products are known for their exceptional build quality, optical performance, and timeless design.

Innovation and Impact:

Leica has been a trailblazer in photographic technology, introducing groundbreaking advancements throughout its history. The Leica M3, introduced in 1954, is a testament to the company's commitment to precision engineering and remains one of the most influential cameras in the industry. Leica continues to push the boundaries of imaging technology with its digital cameras and optical solutions.
